# Spectacularity

So one day, I was walking across this road, right? And on that road, I spotted something. Something insane. Something crazy.

It was A Blog. And man oh man was this blog quite crazy. You see, A Blog is not just any blog. It is the first. For A Blog was the first identifier. Then there was B. Then C. But only A shall remain the first one of all.
